11 Silent Taxi: The Way Together

GIV.O New York and Boston



About

Support for drivers of silent taxis


Issue

There are taxi drivers who are deaf. Silent taxis are special taxis designed for communication between passengers and taxi drivers with hearing impairments. The drivers of silent taxis are leading an independent lives with excellent work skills, breaking the prejudice that their communication and driving will be difficult. The drivers can communicate with passengers and operate more safely through customized tablet PCs. People may have the prejudices unconsciously, but through this project, the world will change.

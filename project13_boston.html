13 The Last Seed: Upcycling Culture Settlement Project

GIV.O Boston



About

For H!Cycle Co. to settle upcycling culture in this world.

Issue

H!Cycle Co. is a social enterprise that provides environmental welfare through upcycling coffee bags and coffee scraps. Upcycle refers to being reborn as a product for a new purpose by adding an eco-friendly design to an abandoned product. Recently, there were natural disasters caused by abnormal weather such as forest fires in Australia and signs of volcanic eruptions in the Philippines. Therefore, the interests in upcycling has rised. Help H!Cycle Co. to settle upcycling culture to this world.

08 Give Honor: Forgotten Heroes

GIV.O New York and Boston



About

For Jong-Hoon Lee, who was a Korean War veteran.


Issue

Jong-Hoon Lee is a veteran of the Korean War, which was the war between South Korea and North Korea. During the Korean War, numerous young people participated and fought bloodlessly for the country, but now it is a forgotten history for many people. Many of the veterans and their descendents are living in a harsh environment these days, without any kind of compensation. Therefore, this project is to think once again and remember the Korean War and its veterans and show some respect for the noble sacrifices of the veterans.

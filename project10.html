GIV.O New York and Boston



About

For a social enterprise "Beautiful Person Co."


Issue

"Beautiful Person Co." is a company that produces wonderful gentlemen's suits. Here, the disabled and the non-disabled work together, compensating for each other's shortcomings. Everyone becomes one in a workplace without any kind of discrimination.

Also, "Beautiful Person Co." tries to actively improve the uncomfortable view of the disabled and to help the disabled work in a better environment.

Support the disabled to take a step closer to the unbiased world.

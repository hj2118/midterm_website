What is GIV.O?

Give Orange (GIV.O) is a non-profit organization by South Korean international students. It is based in New York and Boston, United States. GIV.O New York and GIV.O Boston carry out projects both in separation and collaboration.

Orange in Give Orange is a slang that describes the wealthy in their 20's, symbolizing a negative image of the Korean international students.

The following is an Orange Man, which is a mascot of Give Orange. It symbolizes the orange.



Purpose

GIV.O aims to create social value through establishing an advanced donation culture. It tires to improve the negative image of the orange by donations through various online and offline events. It informs more people of different social problems and tries to establish a culture of small donations by conducting fundraising for many social enterprises.


Why GIV.O?

GIV.O is more accessible and easier to participate in each project as students. Anyone can donate any amount of money. The amount is not the one that matters. The will to donate and help others is the most important thing. The past and ongoing projects by GIV.O are for various groups of people who are in need of help.

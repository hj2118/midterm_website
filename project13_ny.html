13 Let's Togle

GIV.O New York



About

Togle is an organization that fosters marriage migrant women who are experiencing career breaks as foreign language conversation instructors to help them engage in economic activities and self-realization.

Issue

In fact, the majority of talented marriage migrant women are having difficulty finding jobs due to social prejudice. Togle is an organization that started to realize opportunities to become socially and economically independent by hiring migrant women as native speakers. It is not just a service that teaches second languages, but is creating a "global dialogue window" where Koreans and foreigners share and understand each other's languages and cultures.

As director Bong, Joon-ho once said, "If you jump over an inch of barrier, it will open to the global world," please help everyone to naturally accept multiculturalism and coexist beyond language barriers.

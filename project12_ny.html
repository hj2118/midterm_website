12 Cheer Up, Reo

GIV.O New York



About

For 119REO, which is a social enterprise that works for firefighters fighting cancer in South Korea.


Issue

Firefighters are the superheroes of this era who always appear and save people whenever in danger, such as fires, accidents, and disasters. The firefighters devote their lives to hot fires to save other precious lives. They suffer from death or injury while protecting the lives of others. However, firefighters exposed to cancer outbreaks are fighting alone because they are not considered work-related accidents. Therefore, help the firefighters who have saved others' lives and now suffering from cancer.
